    Instructions
    • The filling of Form has to be done from top most column to lower column to avoid errors in calculations
    • Days input in the form refer to Total Number of days you to calculate your attendance for
    • Not Accountable Classes input refer to those classes whose attendance is not considered in your attendance evaluation ,for example library,extra activities etc
